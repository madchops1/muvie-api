What Should I Have In My VJ Gear?

Its tough keeping track of what you need to bring to a set. Here is a list of items you may find useful in your VJ Kit.

  • Windows or Apple OSx laptop to run the Visualz, and other software.
  • Projector (Its nice to have a few for different situations).
  • Projector screen(s) and stand(s).
  • Portable AC power station.
  • Power Strip
  • Zip ties.
  • Extension cords.
  • Apple TV & Remote
  • HDMI cable
  • VGA cable
  • HDMI -> VGA adapter
  • HDMI -> Thunderbolt adapter
  • HDMI -> USB adapter
