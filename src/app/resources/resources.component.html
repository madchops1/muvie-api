How To Do Live Visuals

What you will need:

  • Computer to run the VISUALZ software.
  • Projector, TV screen, or computer monitor.
  • HDMI or VGA cable depending on your hardware.

Other useful things you may or may not need:

  • Projector screen
  • Portable power station

Once you have your gear your ready. Creating a track or a set is easy in VISUALZ. Checkout the Learn Page to see how to do it. But trust me it's very simple.

When you are done composing your set in VISUALZ. Push the play button, and then the fullscreen button. You're doing it.
