Welcome to Visulz. When you open the app click new set. A new track with some content will be created for you automatically. You can toggle between the Track List and Content in the left sidebar.

The audio analyzer is one of the main components to Visualz. Using volume amplitude and peak detection Visualz detects timing to trigger the transition of scenes, and activate fx in your tracks.

If you're ready to learn more checkout the User Guide.
