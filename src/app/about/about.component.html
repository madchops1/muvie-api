ABOUT

VISUALZ is from Chicago, but now we have a presence in Colorado!

The creators of visualz are passionate about coding, technology, lighting, visuals, music & artists.

These passions were combined to create the audio reactive graphics software, VISUALZ. Designed for VJs, videos, events, concerts, live streams, and installations.

Compose your sets in advance or VJ live and in realtime. You can optionally stream your output anywhere in the world over the browser or to OBS or any Smart TV.

VISUALZ is built using web technologies such as WebGL, Web Audio, WebRTC, and other black magic.

When not building software. We're doing festivals, clubs, streams, events, installations, and more. Email contact@visualzstudio.com for anything you need.
