Passionate about technology, visual arts, music & artists. VISUALZ is based in Chicago.

Our founders combined these passions into the audio reactive VJ software platform, VISUALZ. Designed for live streams, events, and installations. Compose your sets ahead of time or VJ in realtime, and optionally stream your output anywhere in the world over the browser or to OBS.

VISUALZ is built using web technologies such as WebGL, Web Audio, WebRTC, and other black magic...
