Sets, Tracks, & Scenes

Sets can have multiple tracks. Your tracks have visual scenes. Scenes are visual media content that can be gifs, images, video, live camera video, graphic visualizers, and more.



Add Scene Content

Add media, camera, fan engagement, and graphic visualizations from this panel.

Add Media

Add videos and media from your computer or search for gifs and images.

Add Camera

Add webcam video or stream mobile video from your smartphone into a visualz scene.

Add FanCam

The FanCam allows your audience to send selfies from their smartphone into the visualz scene by scanning a QR code on the screen.

Add AlphaViz

AlphaViz is an ubervizesque graphic visualizer which you can add additional effects to. All FX may not behave as intended in this scene.

Add Vapor

Vapor is a vaporwave style graphic visualizer. All FX may not behave as intended in this scene.
