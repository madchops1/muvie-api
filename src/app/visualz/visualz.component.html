Features

cast_connected

Projection

Visuals that change to the beat of your music

Visualz is a tool for creating projections that automatically sync and change with the music in realtime.

Immediately create an awesome experience at your show, event, or party with a visual composition that bumps.

camera_front

FanCam

Audience selfies sent to the wall in real time

The FanCam feature is revolutionary for the visuals space. Now your audience can interact by taking a selfie. The image is then sent directly to the projection or wall.

Until now this has been complicated and very expensive. Visualz makes it turn-key for free ;)

mobile_screen_share

CrowdScreen

Mobile device flashlight and screen control

The CrowdScreen feature is cutting-edge technology for the lighting industry. Create amazing visual experiences by incorporating hunderends and thousands of devices.

As with the FanCam this is usually complicated and expensive, requiring hardware. Visualz makes it as easy as clicking a button. Still free.
