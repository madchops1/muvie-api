VISUALZ for DJs

Realtime audio-responsive visual composition software for DJs, VJs, Artists, and Venues.

Features

  • Desktop app for Mac/Windows.
  • Audio-Responsive visuals.
  • Real-time beat/amplitude detection.
  • Compose visuals for your tracks and set.
  • Input via 1/8" Line In or Microphone.
  • Output via HDMI to projector, screen, or LED wall.
  • Convert your own art, graphics, images, and video into video sets.
  • Drag & Drop images, videos, gifs, colors, webcam, text, and all other effects.
  • Realtime oscilloscope effect.
  • Various other effects...
  • Save individual tracks, and complete sets of multiple tracks.
  • Coming Soon: Change the color on your fan's phone at your set in sync with your visuals.
  • Coming Soon: Multiple remote cameras feature.
  • Coming Soon: Allow fans at your set to send live selfies directly to your visuals from their phones.
  • Coming Soon: Built-in code editor for your own custom effects.
  • Coming Soon: Rules to control when content plays during your track, eg. at loud or quiet points.
  • ... Much more is in development. We've got ideas on ideas for the future.
