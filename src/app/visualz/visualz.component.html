VISUALZ for DJs

Free realtime audio-responsive visual composition software for DJs, VJs, Artists, and Venues.

DOWNLOAD FREE

Features

  • sync Real-time beat/amplitude detection and audio-responsive visual playback.
  • view_module Quickly drag & drop to compose visual sets of tracks with videos, images, effects and more.
  • mic Use your built-in microphone or mic input.
  • all_out Output to projector, tvs, LED screens, lighting hardware, mobile devices, and more.
  • mobile_screen_share CrowdScreen feature integrates the flashlight and screen on your audience's mobile devices into your set.
  • flash_on Laserz feature integrates VISUALZ Laserz into your set.
  • linked_camera RemoteCam feature integrates crew cameramen and remote camera angles into your set.
  • camera_front FanCam feature allows members of the audience to interact by taking real-time selfies that are sent to the visual composition.
