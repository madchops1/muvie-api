Audio Analyzer & Performance Monitor

Waveform

The waveform visualizes the current microphone/line-in signal.

Beat Decay Line

When a beat or audio change event is detected this line will decay from that level and must be crossed again before the next change event.

Level Line

Tracks the current mic/line-in level.

Beat/Change Indicator

The background of the analyzer will flash and decay with the beat decay line when there is a beat/change.

Frames Per Second Monitor

Monitors the frames per second of the current render in the preview window. Click for a display of ms to render a single frame.

Estimated BPM

In realtime based on beat detection, decay, and volume amplitude, Visualz will estimate the BPM. This data is used in visualizations.
